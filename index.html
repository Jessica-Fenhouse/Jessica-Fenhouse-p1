Top 5 Dinosaurs

by Jessica Fenhouse
10/16/2019

Over 65 million years ago the largest animals to walk the Earth lived. These animals were the dinosaurs. They can in all shape, colors, and sizes. Today, there are 700 known and named species of dinosaurs. Of these 700, 5 rank at the top in popularity.
